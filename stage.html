Stage

Her first audience was her family, her stage an old rug. Year 2000 marks her first show in a preschool play. Everyone could see her talent for performing when she stood there in a blueberry costume.

Her early years are full of beautiful memories acting in every school play and participating in cultural life of the community. However, it was in the School of Cultural Animators where she gained most experience. It was two years of hard stage work that covered all aspects of play preparation: play selection, screenplay, music, lighting, costume design and preparation, stage design and stage-prop design.
